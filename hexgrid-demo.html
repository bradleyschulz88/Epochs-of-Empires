HexGrid Module Demo

Hex Grid Module Features

  • Easily create hex grids with createHexGrid(width, height, size, origin)
  • Convert between pixel and axial coordinates
  • Find neighboring hexes and calculate distances
  • Generate hex rings and spirals for advanced map generation
  • Support for pointy-top hexagon orientation

Interact with the demo: Hover over hexes to see their coordinates, click to select a hex and see its neighbors.
